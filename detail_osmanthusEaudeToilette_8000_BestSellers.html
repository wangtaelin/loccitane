Osmanthus Eau de Toilette

Elegant, bright and serene fragrance.

Made in France SKU: 11ET075O21 Volume 2.5 fl. oz $80.00

Free Shipping with any $49 purchase & 30 day return policy.

Inspired by the light of the moon, this alluring fragrance evokes both a sense of serenity and intrigue. The naturally elegant scent of osmanthus is complemented with notes of velvety apricot, green freshness and creamy wood, for a delicately floral and fruity blend.

A traditional ingredient beloved for centuries in China, Osmanthus flowers are a symbol of good luck and bloom each autumn when most flowers have faded.
