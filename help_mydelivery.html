MY DELIVERY

Do you provide free shipping for orders going to Alaska, Hawaii, or Puerto Rico?

All orders to these states have a shipping charge of $17.99. We do not offer free shipping at any time for these states.

Do you ship to any U.S. Territories aside from Puerto Rico?

Yes! We ship to the following U.S. Territories: American Samoa, Federated States of Micronesia, Guam, Marshall Islands, Northern Mariana islands, Palau, and the Virgins Islands. Standard shipping to these territories is $4.95. However, we do not have the option to place these orders online at this time, but are happy to do so over the phone at 1-888-623-2880.

When will my order ship?

Orders are processed within 1-2 business days. We do not ship on the weekends or federal holidays. If you place your order on Saturday or Sunday, it will be shipped the following Tuesday. Express and Overnight orders will be processed the same day if the order is placed before 3:00 PM EST on a weekday. Please allow 1 processing day for Express and Overnight orders placed after 3:00 PM EST.

Will all of my items come in one package?

A majority of our orders are shipped in one box. However sometimes the contents of your order are split up into multiple packages to help evenly distribute weight.

My order did not come with the promotional item I redeemed at checkout.

Please make sure that you did not use any other offers at the time of checkout. Promotions are available while supplies last.

Please note that you must enter a promotional code at checkout in order for you to redeem the promotion. At no time do we offer promotions that are automatically applied at checkout, unless you qualify for free shipping. If you are certain we made a mistake, please call us at 1-888-623-2880 or send us an email at contact.en-us@loccitane.com, providing your order ID and the promotional code you tried to use.

Do you have any shipping restrictions?

Yes we do. Some of the products we sell are considered Hazardous Materials (HAZMAT). Hazardous Materials can be: aerosol items, certain alcohol-based items, items that contain contents under pressures, or are potentially flammable. The U.S. Department of Transportation requires that all HAZMAT items are shipped via standard shipping. As some shipping methods involve air travel, there are air shipment restrictions that do not allow HAZMAT items to be flown. Because of this, we are unable to ship HAZMAT items to Alaska, Hawaii, or PR. If you would like to know which products we carry are considered HAZMAT, please send us an email at contact.en-us@loccitane.com. A Customer Care Representative will be happy to provide this information.

Where do you ship to?

We ship to all 50 states and U.S. Territories. We also accept orders going to APO/FPO/DPO addresses. We accept PO Box addresses. We ship to the following U.S. Territories: American Samoa, Federated States of Micronesia, Guam, Marshall Islands, Northern Mariana Islands, Palau, and the Virgins Islands. Standard shipping to these territories is $4.95. However, we do not have the option to place these orders online at this time, but are happy to do so over the phone at 1-888-623-2880.

My tracking information says that my order has been delivered to Dayton, NJ.

If your tracking information states that your order has been delivered to DAYTON, NJ 08810, we regret to inform you that your package has been sent back to our warehouse facility. Packages are considered undeliverable and are often sent back due (but not limited) to incomplete/non-existent addresses, addresses lacking forwarding information, or addresses that may be physically obstructed.

Where is my order shipped from?

All orders are shipped from our warehouse in Dayton, New Jersey.

Which carriers do you use?

We only deliver via USPS, FED-EX or DHL for standard shipping. Unfortunately, we cannot inform you immediately of the carrier that will deliver your order if you select standard shipping for your order. This is because we now choose the most efficient shipping method for your package on a case-by-case basis to ensure that you receive your package promptly.

Please tell me more about free shipping

We offer free shipping to the contiguous US on orders over $49 (before tax). Please note that orders qualifying for free shipping are shipped via Standard shipping and may take up to 10 business days to arrive.

When I track my order it says "delivered" but I have not received it. What should I do?

Do not fret. From time to time carriers will mark packages as “delivered” when they arrive to their local sorting facility. Because of this, we ask that you please allow the full 10 business days for your package to be delivered to you.

Why is my package delayed?

We are sorry to hear that your order is running behind. If the carrier is experiencing a delay, please check with the package's carrier for the latest updates on your order status.
