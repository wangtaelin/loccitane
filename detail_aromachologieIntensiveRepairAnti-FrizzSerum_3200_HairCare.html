Aromachologie Intensive Repair Anti-Frizz Serum

Made in France SKU: 17AF100G20 Normal - 3.3 fl. oz Select Intensive Repair $32.00

Free Shipping with any $49 purchase & 30 day return policy.

Formulated without silicones, this 3-in-1 serum tames frizz, protects it against heat devices up to 440°F (230°C) and makes hair smooth and silky without weighing it down. This creamy serum enriched with essential oils of Angelica, Ylang Ylang, Sweet Orange, Lavender and Geranium is combined with Sunflower, Jojoba and oat amino acids for an enhanced efficacy.
