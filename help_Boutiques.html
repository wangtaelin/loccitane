BOUTIQUES

Are your promotional offers also available in store?

Most of our promotional offers are in-store and online. We recommend that you read the terms & conditions of the promotion you are interested in to ensure that it can be redeemed in-store.

Can I visit the L'Occitane factory?

L'OCCITANE invites you to discover its factory in the heart of Provence in Manosque.

1. Factory visit

A free guided tour of one hour allows you to follow the different stages of production of our cosmetics by way of scent.

2. Museum

An interactive and sensory space traces the milestones in the history of our brand, from the emotion of the first distillation to the actions led by The L'Occitane Foundation. Temporary exhibitions renewed four times a year.

3. Shop

The largest store in France where you can find all the iconic and new products at a preferential tariff plus valuable advice from the sales team.
