If you would like to unsubscribe from our marketing emails, please click on the "unsubscribe" link that is provided at the bottom of the latest L’OCCITANE email you received. Changed your mind and want to receive our emails again? No problem! You can sign up again on our website or call our Customer Care team at 1-888-623-2880.

I forgot my password.

That’s okay, we can get you right back on track. Please select “Forgot Password?” under the login page. You will be emailed a password reset request within 24 hours.
