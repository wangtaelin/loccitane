This after-shave balm leaves a light fragrance on the skin, scented with a mysterious and sensual blend of cedar wood and frankincense. Enriched with shea butter, glycerin of vegetable origin and birch tree extract, this After-Shave Balm helps to: hydrate and soothe the skin after shaving; reduce feelings of irritation and tightness caused by shaving; tone and protect the skin from external aggressions.
