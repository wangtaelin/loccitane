Your anytime, anywhere salve. Add a soothing dab to your lips, face, hands, body…the list goes on. Our multi-purpose balm helps to nourish, protect, and soothe your dry, delicate skin. This unique oil-in-balm formula is made from our best: shea butter, almond oil, immortelle essential oil, and lavender essential oil—all blended to create a piece of Provence in your pocket.

Featured Ingredients

  • Shea Butter

    Rich in Omega 6, Shea Butter nourishes, moisturizes and comforts the skin.

  • SWEET ALMOND OIL

    Rich in omega 6, this almond oil is mostly used for its nourishing powers on dry skin.

  • LAVENDER ESSENTIAL OIL

    Known for its soothing properties and relaxing scent.
