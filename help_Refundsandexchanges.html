REFUNDS AND EXCHANGES

I was gifted a L'OCCITANE product and I would like to exchange it. Can this be done?

If you wish to exchange a gift, please visit our L'OCCITANE boutiques to complete this request (along with your invoice/gift receipt). At this time we are unable to exchange gift returns at our distribution center.

Can I return a product I purchased in another country?

We cannot accept returns for any products purchased outside of the US.

Can I exchange items?

You may exchange both online and in store purchases, in store. For online purchases, you may return your online item/order back to our warehouse and place a new order for the desired product. For more information on our return policy

Where is my refund?

When paying with a credit/debit card, please allow 3-7 business days for your refund to reach your bank account/credit cardt. Amazon Pay refunds should reflect back into your account within 24 hours. PayPal refunds can take up to 30 days.

When returning my order, do I have to pay any shipping fees?

Yes, a fee of $4.95 shipping fee will be deducted from your order once the return has been processed.

Can I return my online order at my local boutique?

Yes. All of our boutiques can accept online returns.

How long will it take for my return to process?

Please allow 2-3 weeks for your return to process once it has arrived at our warehouse facility. Please keep your return tracking number close by to see when it is back in our possession./p>
