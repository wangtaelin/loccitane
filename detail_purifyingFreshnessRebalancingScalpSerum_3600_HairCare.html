Purifying Freshness Rebalancing Scalp Serum

Rebalance and refresh the scalp with this serum.

Made in France SKU: 11SR075P21 Volume 2.5 fl. oz $36.00

Free Shipping with any $49 purchase & 30 day return policy.

Formulated without silicone, this scalp serum works to reduce excess sebum in normal to oily hair. The gel texture absorbs into the scalp without weighing hair down and creates a refreshing effect. Infused with thyme, grapefruit, mint, lavender, and cedar essential oil, delight in the uplifting aroma with each use. The scalp is left feeling rebalanced, comfortable, and soothed.
