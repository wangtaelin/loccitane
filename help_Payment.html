Payment

Does Klarna affect my credit score?

When you choose to pay with Klarna, a soft credit check may be performed. This type of check will not impact your credit score, and it will not show up as a hard inquiry on your credit report.

Klarna: What happens if I pay later? Will I be charged for paying late?

If we are unable to collect a payment on the scheduled day, we will try again. If the payment is unsuccessful after a second attempt, a late fee of up to $7 will be added to your outstanding balance. Missed payments will be added to the total of the next scheduled payment. To keep you in the loop, Klarna will send you an email notification if your payment was unsuccessful. Please keep in mind if payments get unpaid, you might be blocked from using this payment option in the future.

Klarna: How do I pay?

The credit or debit card you designated to create your account will be used to make automatic payments every two weeks. Expect no interest or fees when you pay on time. The initial payment is charged when we complete your order (this is usually the shipping date). The next 3 installment payments are automatically charged every 2 weeks after your first payment. We will provide notice within 2 days after each installment payment confirming that you have paid the installment amount due.

Are there any restrictions on how I can use Klarna?

Klarna is not available in our retail stores and can only be used online. Because of this, Klarna can not be chosen for Buy Online Pick Up In-Store. It is also not available when using Auto-Replenishment or purchasing a gift card.
